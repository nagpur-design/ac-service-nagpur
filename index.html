AC Service and Repair

Our Services

🔧 AC Installation

Our team of experts will install your AC unit with precision and care. We ensure that the installation is done according to the manufacturer's instructions and that the unit is functioning properly.

We also provide maintenance and repair services to ensure that your AC unit continues to function efficiently.

💡 AC Repair

If your AC unit is not functioning properly, our team of experts will diagnose the problem and provide a solution. We use only genuine spare parts to ensure that the repair is done to the highest standard.

We also provide emergency repair services to ensure that you stay cool and comfortable in your home or office.

🔩 AC Maintenance

Regular maintenance is essential to ensure that your AC unit continues to function efficiently. Our team of experts will perform routine maintenance tasks such as cleaning the filter, checking the refrigerant level, and inspecting the unit for any signs of wear and tear.

We also provide customized maintenance plans to suit your specific needs and budget.

🚮 AC Cleaning

A dirty AC unit can reduce its efficiency and increase your energy bills. Our team of experts will clean your AC unit thoroughly, including the filter, coils, and fan.

We use only eco-friendly cleaning products to ensure that the cleaning process is safe for the environment.

💻 AC Troubleshooting

If your AC unit is not functioning properly, our team of experts will diagnose the problem and provide a solution. We use advanced diagnostic tools to identify the root cause of the problem and provide a cost-effective solution.

We also provide preventive maintenance services to ensure that your AC unit continues to function efficiently.

🔋 AC Gas Refilling

If your AC unit is low on refrigerant, our team of experts will refill it to the recommended level. We use only high-quality refrigerant to ensure that the unit functions efficiently.

We also provide gas leakage detection services to ensure that your AC unit is safe and efficient.
